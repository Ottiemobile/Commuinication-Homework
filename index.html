What is Communication?

Communication is a two way process of reaching a mutual understanding in which people who take action not only gain things such as exchange in information, ideas, news, and feelings, but they also create and share meaning. Basically, communication is a way to connect to people or places. In business scenarios, it is used as a function of management. Orginization cannot be operational without the use of communication between levels, organizations, and employees on a daily basis.

    According to American political scientist and communications theorist, Harold Lasswell, he describes in his words on what an act of communication is doing. The questions to ask when looking at an act of communication is:
  • Who?
  • Says what?
  • To whom?
  • In which channel?
  • With what effect?

Effective Communication

A two-way information sharing process which involves one party sending a message that is easily understood today by the receiving party. Effective communication by business managers faciliates info sharing between company employees and can substantially contribute to its commercial success
